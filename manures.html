Rooting Hormone

Rooting hormones are chemical remedies that are applied to the cut end of a stem or branch to stimulate root growth. They are used mainly by gardeners who propagate their plants but also have applications in agriculture and horticulture.

HOW TO USE

1.During propagation, the rooting hormone should be applied immediately before you place your clipping in the soil.
2.Dip the base of the cutting into the hormone.
3.Then shake gently to remove any excess.
4.Place the cutting into moist soil, loosely covering the base.

DAP (Phosphate) Granules (5gm)

DAP (Phosphate) is a rich phosphate-based fertilizer. Phosphorus is an important nutrient along with Nitrogen and plays a crucial role in the development of new plant tissues and the regulation of protein synthesis in products. DAP furnishes phosphorus nutrition throughout the crop growth and development cycle, as well as fulfills the crop's initial requirement of Nitrogen and Sulphur.

HOW TO USE

1.Add the granules to the pot and water the plant. The granules will dissolve gradually and show results in a week.
2.Dissolve the granules in one-liter water and spray the water on the plants and roots. This water can be used for up to 5 pots.
