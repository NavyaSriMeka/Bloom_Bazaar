Beans Seeds (10 seeds)
Rs. 10.00

Beans Seeds

Beans is a leguminous plant that is rich in protein. It is used in fresh or dried form. Try our Non-GMO Beans Seeds.

HOW TO GROW BEANS FROM SEEDS

1.Soak the seeds for 10 - 24 hours before planting.
2.Take a Seedling Tray and fill it with Cocopeat.
3.Make ½ inch deep hole and put 1 seed in each hole.
4.Fill the hole with Cocopeat and spray with water using a spray gun or spray bottle.
5.Water 3 times a week.
6.Seeds will germinate within 4 - 10 days.
7.Full sunlight is needed.
8.Re-pot the sapling to a Medium Pot in 18 - 22 days.
9.The harvest time is 7 weeks.

Capsicum Green Seeds

Capsicum is a herb that has medicinal properties. It is added to many Italian dishes. Try our Non-GMO Capsicum Green Seeds.

HOW TO GROW CAPSICUM GREEN FROM SEEDS

1.Soak the seeds for 4 - 6 hours before planting.br> 2.Take a Seedling Tray and fill it with Cocopeat.
3.Make ½ inch deep hole and put 4 seeds in each hole.
