Sunflower Tall Seeds (20 seeds)
Rs. 20.00

Sunflower Tall Seeds

Sunflower is beautiful flora and provides us with both oil & food. Sunflower has also proven to reduce blood pressure, cholesterol, and blood sugar, as they contain vitamin E, magnesium, and protein. Bright yellow flowers that add a poster-like effect to our garden can now be in your garden. Try our Non-GMO Sunflower Tall Seeds.

HOW TO GROW SUNFLOWER TALL FROM SEEDS

1.Soak the sunflower seeds for 12 - 24 hours in water..
2.Take a Seedling tray and fill it with Cocopeat.
3.Make ½ inch deep hole and put 2 seeds in each hole.
4.Fill the hole with Cocopeat and spray with water using a spray gun or spray bottle.
5.Sow the seeds vertically on the sprouting side.
6.Keep the soil moist, not soggy.
7.Seeds will germinate within 7 - 10 days.
8.Full sunlight is needed.

Daisy Mixed Seeds

Add the colorful blooming heads to your garden adding aesthetic value to it. Daisies come in a vast array of colors and forms, from small bedding daisies to plate-sized blossoms. Try our Non-GMO Daisy Mixed Seeds.

HOW TO GROW DAISY FROM SEEDS

1.Take a Seedling Tray and fill it with Cocopeat.
2.Make a hole double the size of the seed.

4.Keep the soil moist not soggy.
5.Seeds will germinate within a week.
6.Re-pot the sapling to a Medium Size Pot in about 2 weeks.
7.Full sunlight is needed.
